Who We are

Envision. Strategize. Execute.

We help brand owners develop and execute global strategies to grow their businesses smarter and more profitably.

What We Do

Strategy
A fresh perspective leads to an executable plan.

The best decisions aren’t made in a vacuum. A thorough study of the strengths and weaknesses of your company or brand, an assessment of the competitive playing field and an understanding of the relevant industry and environmental trends leads to a go-forward plan. The first step is understanding your short and long term needs and wants. Fact-based strategic thinking leads to a plan for value creation.

Execution

We link our success to yours.

Our engagement may extend to finding alliance partners, facilitating licensing agreements, buy/sell side advisory, assistance in strategic planning, marketing and commercial development of new products. Our collaborative network of professionals span a broad spectrum of disciplines, industries and channels across the globe. Together, we’ll find an optimal, cost-effective means of implementing your strategy.

Transactions

Our approach is focused, narrow and surgical – based upon solid research and analysis, industry knowledge and trusted business relationships.

Hilltop provides industry-specific origination and buy side advisory to companies seeking acquisitions and joint ventures in the fashion and home goods sectors. On the sell-side, smaller companies benefit from the Hilltop Alliance’s expertise to position them for the market, discreetly identify potential buyers and negotiate favorable terms and conditions of sale.

Global Connectivity
Think local and global. Act local and global.

Internationalization isn’t easy without in-depth local market knowledge and a reliable partner on the ground to realize your vision.


Our professional network is global and our collective business experience spans the continents. Whether you are looking to expand into emerging markets, such as India and the Middle East, or mature markets such as Western Europe or the U.S., Hilltop may be your best first call to explore foreign expansion options.
